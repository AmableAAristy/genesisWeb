About us

Welcome to Genesis Children’s Academy of Florida for the 2024-2025 school year! We are thrilled to have you join our community and appreciate the trust you place in us for your child’s education and well-being.

At Genesis Children’s Academy of Florida, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and positive environment. We are a state-licensed preschool, and our curriculum follows the “Creative Curriculum” framework to foster your child’s growth and learning.

We collaborate closely with various agencies, including CDS, VPK, the Early Learning Coalition of Miami-Dade, and Step Up for Students, to ensure the best resources and support for our students. Our commitment is to offer a safe, loving, and encouraging atmosphere for every child in our care.

We look forward to an exciting and enriching school year with you and your child!
