Our Curriculum

At Genesis Children’s Academy of Florida, we proudly implement the Creative Curriculum, a comprehensive, research-based curriculum designed to foster children's development and learning. The Creative Curriculum emphasizes exploration and discovery as a way of learning, enabling children to develop confidence, creativity, and lifelong critical thinking skills.

Key Features of the Creative Curriculum:

  • Child-Centered Learning: Focuses on the interests and developmental levels of each child, encouraging them to explore and engage with their environment.
  • Active Exploration: Promotes hands-on learning through play, allowing children to investigate and learn about the world around them.
  • Developmentally Appropriate Practices: Adapts to the needs of children at different stages of development, ensuring that each child is challenged and supported.
  • Integrated Learning: Combines various areas of learning, including language, literacy, math, science, social studies, and the arts, to provide a holistic educational experience.
  • Family Engagement: Encourages strong partnerships with families, recognizing their crucial role in their child’s learning and development.

    • Our goal with the Creative Curriculum is to create a dynamic and nurturing environment where children feel safe, loved, and eager to learn. By focusing on the whole child, we aim to support their cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development, preparing them for future academic success and a love of lifelong learning.
